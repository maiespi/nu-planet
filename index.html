About

NuPlanet is an application that assists users in leading a healthy and balanced lifestyle while implementing a bit of water conservation into their practical day.

NuPlanet also uses location services to help find cheap, healthy food choices closest to one’s current location. NuPlanet also helps the user to find nearby "sweet treats" for a cheat day.

Not only does NuPlanet provide people with healthier food options, but it also compares a typical diet to the suggested options. For instance, if someone was to buy a hamburger, NuPlanet would inform the user that in choosing to buy a veggie burger instead, they would save up to 660 gallons of water.

Inspiration

Healthy Diet

Eating healthy isn’t always easy, but committing to a healthy diet can be one of the smartest decisions you ever make. Why? Not only can eating well make you look and feel better, it can also save you money on future health costs.

Eating well also means leaving out or only rarely consuming foods that are high in added sugar, saturated fat and sodium. That includes most fast food, full-calorie sodas, processed snacks like chips and crackers, and anything with more milligrams of sodium than there are calories in a serving.
